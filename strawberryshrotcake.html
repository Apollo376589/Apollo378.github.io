Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Layers of fluffy cake, fresh strawberries, and whipped cream. A delightful and fruity dessert option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine sliced strawberries and 1/4 cup sugar. Let them sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour a baking dish.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined.
  5. Spread the batter in the prepared baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let the cake cool completely. Cut it into squares.
  7. Assemble the shortcakes by layering cake squares with sliced strawberries and whipped cream.
