Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

Decadent chocolate cake with a gooey molten center. A heavenly treat for chocolate lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup semisweet or bittersweet chocolate chips
  • 1 and 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour four ramekins or custard cups.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate chips together. Stir until smooth.
  3. Stir in the powdered sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  5. Stir in the flour and a pinch of salt until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-14 minutes, or until the sides are set but the center is still soft.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Run a knife around the edges and invert the cakes onto plates.
  9. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately.
