Guacamole Recipe

A classic Mexican dip made with ripe avocados, tomatoes, onions, and lime juice. Perfect for parties or as a snack.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Add diced onion, diced tomatoes, and lime juice to the bowl.
  3. Mash everything together with a fork until desired consistency is reached.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
