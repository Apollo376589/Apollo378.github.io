Vegetarian Stir-Fry Recipe

Colorful mix of fresh vegetables stir-fried to perfection. A quick and healthy vegetarian option.

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fresh vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add assorted fresh vegetables and stir-fry until they are cooked but still crisp.
  4. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Vegetarian Stir-Fry!
