Chicken Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Creamy Alfredo sauce paired with grilled chicken and fettuccine pasta. A comforting and satisfying main course.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill chicken until fully cooked, then slice into strips.
  3. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  4. In a saucepan, melt butter and add heavy cream, stirring continuously.
  5. Add grated Parmesan cheese to the sauce, stirring until well combined.
  6. Combine grilled chicken strips with the Alfredo sauce.
  7. Serve the chicken Alfredo over cooked fettuccine pasta.
